Latitude vs Max Temperature



This scatter plot displays a cities Latitude VS. Max Temperature. We can instantly see a trend by how the plots are in a line that curves. We can also observe that temperatures begin to rise as the cities approach the equator (0 latitude).

However, the highest temperatures were reached within 20 degrees of the equator. Also, the max temperatures were located in the Southern Hemisphere. Based on this plot, we can state that on October 19, 2020, the closer a city was to the equator, the warmer it was.
