Summary: Latitude vs. X

Do you believe that the closer a city is to the equator, the warmer the weather? How do you know if this is true or not? Well, in this project, thatâ€™s what we set out to discover. Thanks to the OpenWeatherMap API, we retrieved data for 1,3000 cities.

Cleaning the data is essential to ensure that the analysis included only cities with temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed data. With a clean dataset of now 517 cities, we created visualizations with Matplotlib to compare latitude vs. weather. On this site, you will find the source data, visualizations created and used in the analysis, plus some explanations along the way. Enjoy exploring what we discovered.
